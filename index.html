An Integrative Approach

Integrative medicine is an approach to care that puts the patient at the center and addresses the full range of physical, emotional, mental, social, spiritual and environmental influences that affect a person’s health. By shifting the traditional disease-centered focus of medical practice to a more patient-centered approach, integrative medicine addresses the whole person, not just an isolated set of symptoms.
